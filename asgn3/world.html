Assignment 3: Creating a Virtual World (Hard)
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

FPS: --

There was a time these lands were peaceful... home even. You miss the valley's music and sounds of life and community. But it's been so long since you've seen another soul in this "damned" valley.

You know you must travel until you find some sign of humanity and pray it doesn't attack you. You only have your yourself and your spirit guardian. That guardian is the last remnant of your home, your wife, your old life....

You must keep moving...

Controls:

  • W: Move Forward
  • A: Move Left
  • S: Move Backward
  • D: Move Right
  • Q: Turn Left
  • E: Turn Right



  • Z: Move Vertical Up
  • X: Move Vertical Down
  • Mouse Hold & Drag: Look Around
  • F: Add Block in Front
  • G: Delete Block in Front
